Mercedes-Benz (German pronunciation: [mɛʁˌtseːdəs ˈbɛnts, -dɛs -] ⓘ),[6][7]
commonly referred to as Mercedes and sometimes as Benz, is a German
luxury and commercial vehicle automotive brand established in 1926. Mercedes-Benz AG (a Mercedes-Benz
Group subsidiary established in 2019) is headquartered in Stuttgart, Baden-Württemberg, Germany.
